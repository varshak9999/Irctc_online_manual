Terms and Conditions of Online Ticket Booking

The IRCTC Portal provides a platform for users to interact with the Indian Railway's Passenger Reservation System (PRS) via the internet. All transactions conducted through this portal are governed by the reservation and booking rules established by Indian Railways, in addition to the specific terms and conditions outlined herein for internet-based bookings.

By registering on the IRCTC website and transacting through this platform, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms and conditions. If you do not agree with these terms, you are prohibited from transacting on this website. Clicking the 'I Agree' button signifies your formal agreement with IRCTC for conducting transactions on this website.

1. User Registration:

  • Each user is permitted to register only once on the IRCTC website. Single user registration is restricted to a unique Email ID and Mobile Number.
  • Multiple User IDs registered under the same Email ID and Mobile Number may result in the deactivation of all associated User IDs.
  • IRCTC reserves the right to deactivate any User IDs created in violation of these terms (e.g., registering more than one User ID or booking tickets using multiple User IDs) and to cancel any tickets booked under such registrations without prior notice.
  • Incomplete or inaccurate registration details, the inclusion of irrelevant or invalid information, or engagement in suspicious booking activities (including the use of scripting tools or unauthorized Tatkal software) may lead to the deactivation of the user account and the cancellation of any associated bookings without explanation. In such cases of misuse, no refund of the ticket fare will be provided.
  • IRCTC reserves the right to deactivate User IDs associated with incorrectly submitted addresses during registration.
  • Users are advised to maintain a maximum of six (6) passengers in their Master Passengers List. Exceeding this limit may render the Master Passengers List inaccessible. However, users can still book tickets for additional passengers by manually entering their details on the Passenger Reservation Page.
  • The IRCTC Website and Mobile Applications are intended for personal, non-commercial use only. The use of automation software and/or scripting software is strictly prohibited. Any unauthorized commercial procurement and supply of Railway tickets using illegal automation or scripting software may result in the deactivation of all involved user accounts and the cancellation of any tickets booked through these User IDs in the PRS, without any prior notification. No refund will be issued for tickets booked through such unauthorized means.
