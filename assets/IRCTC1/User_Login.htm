User Login

Logging into Your IRCTC Account

Once your registration is complete and your account has been successfully created, you can proceed to log in using your registered credentials. The login interface, as depicted below, requires the following information for successful authentication:

  1. Username: Enter your designated username in the provided field.
  2. Password: Input your chosen password in the corresponding password field. Ensure that the Caps Lock is off, as the password field is case-sensitive.
  3. Captcha: Accurately enter the alphanumeric characters displayed in the image within the designated "Captcha" field. This security measure helps prevent automated access.

Upon entering the valid username, password, and the correct captcha, click the "Login" button to access your IRCTC account.
