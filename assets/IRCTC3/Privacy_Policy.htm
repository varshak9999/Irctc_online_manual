This document outlines the policies and procedures implemented by Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation Limited (IRCTC) to ensure the privacy and security of its users' personal and financial information across its website (www.irctc.co.in) and mobile applications. IRCTC is committed to employing measures designed to safeguard user data.

1. General Data Collection Practices:

As a standard practice, this website and its mobile applications do not automatically collect Personal Information when users visit the site. Users can generally navigate the platform without disclosing personal details unless they voluntarily choose to provide such information.

2. Commitment to User Trust and Non-Commercial Use:

IRCTC is dedicated to maintaining the trust and confidence of visitors to its website (www.irctc.co.in) and mobile applications. Users are permitted to access and download information or materials from these platforms solely for personal and non-commercial purposes. Any unauthorized or illegal use of the website or mobile application is strictly prohibited.

3. Social Media Integration:

The IRCTC eTicketing platform may utilize social media networks (e.g., Facebook, Twitter) to disseminate information and videos for the enhanced convenience of commuters and the general public. IRCTC assumes no responsibility and shall not be liable for any errors or inconveniences encountered while browsing these social media platforms, or for any objections raised by the hosting organizations/agencies at any time.

4. Site Visit Data Logging:

This website and mobile application record user visits and log the following information for statistical analysis: IP address, browser type, operating system type, access date and time, accessed webpages, and downloaded files. This data may be utilized to analyze aggregate traffic patterns.

The IRCTC eTicketing website and mobile applications do not automatically capture specific personal information (such as name, phone number, or email address) that would allow individual user identification.

5. Use of Cookies:

This site employs cookies to personalize the user experience and ensure effective website functionality. Cookies are utilized to identify user interests in IRCTC products and to track the efficacy of advertising campaigns. This process does not involve the collection of any personal information.

6. Browser Cookie Management:

Most web browsers provide users with the ability to view, delete cookies on an individual or website basis, or block cookies from specific or all websites. Cookies can enhance user convenience by automatically logging users in (requiring only password entry). IRCTC may also use cookies to display advertisements or send targeted offers (via email, provided users have not opted out) based on potential user interests.

Users should be aware that deleting cookies will result in the loss of any customized preferences, including opt-out preferences for cookies, as this function relies on the presence of an opt-out cookie. Deleting cookies may also lead to the unavailability of certain services.

7. Automatic Session Data Logging:

Each access to the IRCTC eTicketing website or mobile applications results in the logging of session data. This data may include the user's IP address, operating system, browser software type, and user activities performed on the platform. Session data is collected to analyze user behavior, browsing patterns (including visit frequency and duration), diagnose server issues, and improve system administration. This information does not personally identify individual users; however, it may be possible to determine a user's Internet Service Provider (ISP) and approximate geographic location based on this data.

8. Links to External Websites/Portals:

The IRCTC eTicketing website and mobile applications may contain hyperlinks to external websites or portals. These links are provided for user information and convenience. Upon selecting a link to an external portal, users will leave the IRCTC platform and become subject to the privacy and security policies of the external website's owners/sponsors. IRCTC is not responsible for the content and reliability of these linked websites and does not necessarily endorse the views expressed therein. The presence of a link on the IRCTC website or mobile application should not be interpreted as an endorsement of any kind. IRCTC does not guarantee the compliance of linked websites with Indian laws and has no control over their availability.

9. Types of Information Collected:

  • Registration Information: When subscribing to or registering on the website or mobile application, users may be required to provide personal identity information (e.g., name, gender, marital status, age, date of birth, nationality) and contact details (e.g., email address, postal address, contact number).
  • Other Information: IRCTC may also collect the following information:
    • Transactional history (excluding banking details) related to e-commerce activities and buying behavior.
    • Data created by the user or a third party that the user chooses to store on IRCTC servers (e.g., image files, documents).
    • Information pertaining to other travelers for whom bookings are made through the user's registered IRCTC account. In such cases, the user must confirm and represent that each traveler has agreed to the disclosure of their information to IRCTC and its subsequent sharing with relevant service providers.

10. Use of Personal Information:

IRCTC may utilize user Personal Information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Confirming reservations with service providers.

  • Providing transaction status updates.

  • Sending booking confirmations via SMS, WhatsApp, or other messaging services.

  • Communicating booking updates or changes.

  • Enabling customer service to contact users if necessary.

  • Customizing website, mobile site, and mobile app content.

  • Requesting product or service reviews and other feedback.

  • Sending verification messages or emails.

  • Validating and authenticating user accounts to prevent misuse or abuse.

11. Surveys:

IRCTC values user opinions and may conduct online and offline surveys. Participation in these surveys is voluntary. Collected information is typically aggregated and used to improve the website, mobile applications, services, and to develop relevant content, features, and promotions based on survey results. Unless explicitly stated otherwise in the survey, the identity of participants remains anonymous.

12. Marketing Promotions, Research, and Programs:

Marketing promotions, research, and programs are conducted to understand user preferences, develop relevant programs, and enhance user experience. Personal Information collected for these activities may include contact details and survey responses. This information is used to develop promotions and product improvements. Registered users may periodically receive updates about product discounts, special offers, new IRCTC services, and other relevant information (e.g., savings on airfares, hotel reservations, holiday packages, and other travel services) and marketing programs.

Users may also receive periodic marketing emails, newsletters, and exclusive promotions featuring special deals.

IRCTC may introduce or enhance services on its platforms. To the extent these services are utilized, provided Personal Information will be used to facilitate the requested services (e.g., responding to email inquiries). This information may also be stored to improve the website and mobile applications for future use.

13. Duration of Personal Information Retention:

IRCTC will retain Personal Information on its servers for a period deemed reasonably necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. In certain circumstances, Personal Information may be retained for longer durations to comply with legal, regulatory, tax, or accounting requirements.

14. Sharing of Personal Information:

  1. Suppliers and Service Providers: User information may be shared with end service providers (e.g., Railways) or other suppliers responsible for fulfilling bookings. By making a booking with IRCTC, users authorize the sharing of their information with these providers for service fulfillment purposes only. IRCTC does not authorize these providers to use user information for any other purposes. The usage of shared information by these independent data controllers is beyond IRCTC's purview and control, and IRCTC cannot be held accountable for their practices. Users are advised to review the privacy policies of the respective service providers whose services they choose to utilize.

    IRCTC does not sell or rent individual customer names or other Personal Information to third parties, except for sharing with business/alliance partners or vendors engaged by IRCTC for referral services and promotional benefits based on users' booking history.

  2. Companies within the Same Group: To enhance personalization and service efficiency, IRCTC may share Personal Information with its affiliates or associate entities under controlled and secure conditions. This enables the provision of information about relevant products and services or assistance with booking-related inquiries.

    In the event of business expansion, development, restructuring, or any other reason, if IRCTC decides to sell, transfer, or assign its business, any part thereof, subsidiaries, or business units, customer information, including Personal Information, may be transferred accordingly as part of such restructuring.

  3. Disclosure of Information: In addition to the aforementioned circumstances, IRCTC may disclose User's Personal Information if legally required, mandated by an enforcement authority for investigation, by court order, or in response to any legal process. Disclosure may also occur to conduct business operations, for regulatory compliance, internal audits, to secure IRCTC systems, or to enforce or protect the rights or properties of IRCTC or its affiliates, associates, employees, directors, or officers. Disclosure may also be necessary when there is reason to believe that it is needed to identify, contact, or take legal action against someone who may be interfering with IRCTC's rights or properties (intentionally or otherwise) or when such activities could potentially harm others.

    Such disclosure and storage may occur without user notification, and IRCTC shall not be liable to the user or any third party for any damages arising from such disclosure and storage.

15. Feedback Option:

IRCTC provides users with the option to share their experiences through reviews, ratings, and general poll questions. Customers can also submit complaints regarding IRCTC services. Participation in the feedback process is voluntary, but users may receive emails or notifications (app, SMS, or other messaging services) to solicit reviews or answers to questions posted by IRCTC. Written reviews may be visible on social media or travel media platforms.

16. Promotional Emails – Option to Unsubscribe:

IRCTC may occasionally send promotional emails about special packages, new initiatives, and special offers. Users who do not wish to receive these emails can unsubscribe or opt out by clicking the designated link or following the instructions provided in each email message.

17. Protection of Personal Information:

All payment transactions on the website and mobile applications are secured. IRCTC employs stringent security measures to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of information under its control. Secure server technology is utilized when users change or access their account information. Once information is in IRCTC's possession, strict security guidelines are adhered to in order to protect it against unauthorized access.

18. Age Limit for Transactions:

Users must be at least 18 years of age to transact directly with IRCTC and to provide consent for the processing of their personal data.

19. Changes to Terms, Conditions, Disclaimer, and Privacy Policy:

IRCTC reserves the right to modify the terms and conditions, disclaimer, and privacy policy outlined herein at any time. Changes or amendments to the privacy policy may be implemented in accordance with management policy norms or Government of India guidelines.

By accessing the IRCTC portal, users acknowledge and agree to be bound by the current terms and conditions, privacy policy, and disclaimer. Users are advised to review these documents each time they revisit the portal.

20. Purpose of Information:

The information contained within the IRCTC website and mobile applications is provided solely for the purpose of disseminating information about IRCTC in the public interest.

21. Limitation of Liability and Governing Law:

By accessing the IRCTC website and mobile applications, users agree that IRCTC shall not be liable for any direct or indirect loss of any nature whatsoever arising from the use or inability to use the platform and its content or any linked websites. The use of the website and mobile applications and any related issues shall be governed strictly by the laws in force in India, and by using these platforms, users submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Delhi/New Delhi.
